The universe

The universe is made up of all matter and space that exists.
In our universe, there are billions of stars. A star goes through a life cycle, just like a human, plant, or animal.
The steps of the cycle just look a little different. One step of the life cycle is the supernova stage.



Binary system

A binary system is made of two stars. When one of them is a compact star called a white dwarf,
it can pull matter from the other star in the binary system. It continues to pull until it explodes. When it explodes, it is called a supernova.
