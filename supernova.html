What's a "Supernova"?

A "Supernova" is an exploding star. Supernovas can happen for 2 different reasons:
1. In a binary star system (a white dwarf and another star.), if the white dwarf pulls too much it causes it to explode.
2. When a dying star's gravity becomes too much, the dying star collapses into itself and explodes.
