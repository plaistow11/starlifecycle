Nuclear Fusion

My definition of nuclear fusion is when a large cloud of dust and gas collapses in on itself until it becomes so dense and hot that the dust and gas particles start sticking.

ANS Center for Nuclear Science and Technology Information's definition (click here to go there) of nuclear fusion is: It is a nuclear process, where energy is produced by smashing together light atoms. It is the opposite reaction of fission, where heavy isotopes are split apart. Fusion is the process by which the sun and other stars generate light and heat.
